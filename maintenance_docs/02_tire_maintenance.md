# TIRE MAINTENANCE GUIDELINES

## Overview
Tires are the only contact point between the vehicle and the road. Worn tires dramatically increase stopping distance, reduce handling, and raise the risk of blowouts — especially at highway speeds or in wet conditions.

## Condition Classification

### New
- Tread depth: 7-9mm
- Manufactured within the last 12 months
- No cracks, bulges, or uneven wear
- Next inspection: at 10,000 km or every 6 months

### Good
- Tread depth: 3-7mm
- Even wear pattern across the tread
- No sidewall damage
- Next inspection: at 5,000 km or every 3 months
- Rotate tires if not done in the last 10,000 km

### Worn Out
- **SAFETY ISSUE** — requires service
- Tread depth: below 2mm (legal minimum in most regions is 1.6mm)
- May show uneven wear, cracking, or sidewall damage
- Hydroplaning risk in wet weather is severe
- **Action: Replace within 1 week. Avoid highway speeds and wet-weather driving until replaced.**

## Service Intervals by Vehicle Type

| Vehicle Type | Typical Tire Life |
|---|---|
| Motorcycle | 15,000 – 25,000 km |
| Car | 40,000 – 60,000 km |
| SUV / Van | 50,000 – 70,000 km |
| Truck / Bus | 60,000 – 100,000 km (proper rotation required) |

## Age-Based Risk Factors

Tires degrade with age even when unused:
- **0–3 years**: normal operation
- **4–6 years**: begin annual sidewall inspection for cracks
- **7+ years**: replace regardless of tread depth — rubber compound degrades

## Typical Service Actions

- **Tire rotation**: every 10,000 km, standard preventive maintenance
- **Wheel alignment**: required when tire condition becomes "Worn Out" unevenly
- **Tire balancing**: required any time new tires are mounted
- **Full tire replacement**: when condition is "Worn Out" or tires are 7+ years old

## Decision Matrix

| Tire Condition | Vehicle Age | Odometer | Priority | Timeline |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Worn Out | Any | Any | HIGH | Within 1 week |
| Good | 7+ years | Any | MEDIUM | Within 1 month |
| Good | Any | 80,000+ km | MEDIUM | Within 1 month |
| Good | 0–6 years | < 80,000 km | LOW | Next scheduled service |
| New | Any | Any | NONE | Rotation only if due |

## Safety Notes
- Never mix worn and new tires on the same axle
- Replace in pairs at minimum; full sets preferred for AWD/4WD vehicles
- Check tire pressure monthly — underinflation causes rapid wear and reduces fuel efficiency
